# Project C — Revolved / Turned Part

**Software:** FreeCAD 1.1 — Part Design & Sketcher workbenches

A rotationally symmetric part created by revolving a 2D profile sketch around an axis — the FreeCAD equivalent of a lathe operation. Parts like this are common in mechanical assemblies: shafts, caps, bushings, and fittings. A Pocket is added after the revolution to cut an internal feature into the face.

## Operations
| Step | Operation | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | Sketch (profile) | Half-profile drawn on the XZ plane |
| 2 | Revolution | Rotate 360° around axis to form solid |
| 3 | Pocket | Internal cut on the face |
